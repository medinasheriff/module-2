The coolest creature

The PARROT is awesome because it is colourful and very intelligent..
Parrots, also known as psittacines, are birds of almost 400 species,
mostly found in tropical and subtropical regions, but sadly, many of them have
become endangered.

Life Cycle of Parrot

A baby parrot goes through five basic developmental stages:

  1. Neonate or hatchling
  2. Nestling
  3. Fledgling
  4. Weanling, and
  5. Juvenile or pre-adolescent.

    Some Cool Facts about Parrots

    • As a famous research subject, the African grey parrot Alex was said to
      have the intelligence of a human 5-year-old.
    • Most of the known species live in the tropical and subtropical regions
      of Australia, Asia, Central and South America and Africa.
    • Parrots are believed to be one of the most intelligent bird species.
    • Many parrots can imitate sounds.
    • Most species of parrots are able to use their feet to manipulate
      food into their mouths, much like the way humans use their hands.
    • Parrot beaks are not only large, they are also strong.
