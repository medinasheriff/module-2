FRIEND OR FOE

With deafening silence you move,
a walk through you is filled with uncertainties,
for you are a double-edgedsword.
You give hope, yet, you cast fear.
You ease pains, but,
you pass and leave agony in your wake

Over you, dreams die.
Over you, wishes come true.
When you slowly speed by,
you neither stop nor linger
oh time !
Are you a Friend or a Foe?

By Medina F. Sheriff
